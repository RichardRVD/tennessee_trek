Welcome to Tennessee Trekker

Here at Tennessee Trekker, we aim to provide you with the highest quality tours you've ever been on. Whether your interest is kayaking, hiking, camping, etc. We want to show you the natural beauty of this state and aim to offer you more than just a tour. We want you to have fun. We want you to become a member of the Trekker community. We want you to be a part of our family and keep coming back for more.


Come book a trip with us today and let us show you the difference between Tennessee Trekker and all the rest. All members who book a trip with Tennessee Trekker will receive a 10% off coupon to our merchandise shop, that way you can remember your trip and show off your skills all at the same time! Sign up to join our family today!
